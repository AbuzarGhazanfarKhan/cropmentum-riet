A New Paradigm for REITs

This model addresses the core inefficiencies of traditional real estate investment by leveraging the power of tokenization and fractionalization. The following comparison highlights the key advantages.

Traditional REIT Equity

  • ●
    High Minimum Investment: Often requires $25,000+ for institutional access.
  • ●
    Slow Settlement: Trades settle in T+2 or T+3 days.
  • ●
    Manual Dividends: Payments are processed manually via checks or ACH, typically quarterly.
  • ●
    Limited Liquidity: Subject to market hours and brokerage limitations.

Tokenized REIT Equity

  • ●
    Low Entry Barrier: Fractional ownership starting at a $50.00 Base Price.
  • ●
    Instant Settlement: Trades settle in near real-time (T+0) on the blockchain.
  • ●
    Automated Dividends: Income is distributed instantly and automatically via smart contract.
  • ●
    Global 24/7 Liquidity: Trade tokens anytime on digital asset exchanges.

How It Works: The Investment Lifecycle

The model operates on a hybrid architecture, combining traditional financial processes with the efficiency of on-chain automation. Explore the two core functions of the tokenized asset: earning income and trading ownership.

Automated Income Distribution Flow

1. Rental Income Flow (Off-Chain)

Tenants of the industrial warehouse pay rent in fiat currency (e.g., USD) to the Operating Company's traditional bank account.

2. Cash Conversion & Transfer (Hybrid)

The OpCo sends the net distributable income to a licensed payment gateway, which converts the fiat into a regulated stablecoin (e.g., USDC).

Or the cash is stored in the bank(e.g., USD or PKR).

3. Smart Contract Activation (On-Chain)

The smart contract verifies receipt of the stablecoins and automatically triggers the Pro-Rata Distribution function, calculating each token holder's share.

4. Instant Payout

Stablecoins are instantly and automatically transferred to each investor's digital wallet, completing the dividend payment process.

OR Transactions are instantly initiated via banks in case of Cash
